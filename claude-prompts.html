Getting Started

FIND YOUR FIRST $1,000 SKILL

Analyze my background: [list your skills/jobs/hobbies]. Identify the top 3 skills I could monetize THIS WEEK. For each, give me: who pays for it, what to charge, and the exact first step to land a client.

This prompt helps you identify monetizable skills you already possess and creates an actionable roadmap to start earning immediately.

Why it works

People pay $500-$2,000/month for skills you already have.

Freelancing

BUILD A FREELANCE OFFER IN 10 MINUTES

Create a high-converting freelance service offer for [your skill]. Include: a compelling package name, what's included, pricing tiers (starter/pro/premium), and a one-paragraph pitch I can paste into Upwork or cold DMs.

Most freelancers fail because their offer is weak. This prompt helps you craft a compelling, structured service offering that converts.

Why it works

Most freelancers fail because their offer is weak. This fixes it.

Sales

WRITE YOUR COLD OUTREACH SEQUENCE

Write a 3-message cold outreach sequence to [target client type] offering [your service]. Message 1: curiosity hook. Message 2: social proof + value. Message 3: low-risk CTA. Keep each under 80 words.

Create a professional cold email sequence that warms up prospects and drives responses without being spammy.

Why it works

Agencies charge $3,000+ to write this for you. Claude does it free.

Products

CREATE A DIGITAL PRODUCT FROM SCRATCH

I know a lot about [your niche/skill]. Help me design a digital product (template, guide, or mini-course) I can sell for $27-$97. Give me: the title, 5 key sections, what transformation it promises, and where to sell it.

Turn your knowledge into a sellable digital asset with clear structure, pricing, and market positioning.

Why it works

One good digital product = income while you sleep.

Strategy

REVERSE-ENGINEER A PROFITABLE NICHE

Research the top 5 most profitable online niches for a solo creator in 2025. For each, show me: average income potential, easiest entry point, best platform to monetize on, and one underserved angle I could own.

Skip months of trial and error by identifying proven, profitable niches with clear paths to monetization.

Why it works

Picking the right niche saves you 6 months of wasted effort.

Marketing

BUILD A CONTENT STRATEGY THAT ATTRACTS CLIENTS

I'm a [your role] targeting [your audience] on [platform]. Create a 30-day content plan designed to attract inbound leads. Include: 4 content pillars, 2 post ideas per pillar per week, and a weekly CTA rotation.

Replace cold calling with warm inbound leads through strategic content that positions you as an authority.

Why it works

Content is the new cold call. This is your script.

Recurring Revenue

TURN YOUR KNOWLEDGE INTO A PAID NEWSLETTER

Help me launch a paid newsletter about [your topic]. Include: a compelling name, a one-sentence value prop, 3 free vs. paid tier breakdowns, suggested price point, and the outline for my first 3 paid issues.

Build predictable recurring revenue by packaging your expertise into a subscription newsletter format.

Why it works

100 subscribers at $10/month = $1,000/month recurring.

Planning

MAP OUT A 90-DAY INCOME PLAN

I'm starting from $0 online. My skills are [list them]. Create a realistic 90-day income plan broken into 3 phases: (1) First $500, (2) First $2,000/month, (3) First $5,000/month. Include specific actions for each phase.

Get a clear, phase-by-phase roadmap from zero to consistent monthly income with specific milestones.

Why it works

Stop guessing. Start executing with a real roadmap.

Systems

AUTOMATE YOUR FOLLOW-UP SYSTEM

Build me a simple follow-up system for freelance leads. Include: a CRM tracker template (text format), follow-up message templates for days 3, 7, and 14, and a re-engagement message for cold leads after 30 days.

Never lose a potential client to forgetfulness with a systematic follow-up process that converts leads.

Why it works

80% of sales happen after the 5th follow-up. Most people quit at 1.

Branding

CREATE YOUR PERSONAL BRAND IN ONE HOUR

Help me build a personal brand around [your skill/story]. Give me: a one-line bio, a content POV statement, 3 brand pillars, my ideal client avatar, and a 'signature story' framework I can use in my content.

Establish a memorable personal brand that attracts your ideal clients and differentiates you from competitors.

Why it works

People buy from people. Your brand is your moat.
